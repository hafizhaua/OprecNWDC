What is Frontend and Backend?

Front-end and Back-end are complementary in web developing.

    Front-end is literally something related to "front", meaning it can be said as developing what users will see in "front" of (in this context) a website or browser. Hence, it's usually called as "client-side" development. It's basically developing what clients/users will see or interact with on a website.

    On the other hand, Back-end is "server-side" development. Ever curious about where your submitted data of presensi go? Back-end is basically the one who manages such data flows. It develops everything related to servers and databases. Hence, it can be said as the "engine" of a website.
